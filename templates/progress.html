What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a value derived from a person's weight and height. It is a useful indicator of whether you're at a healthy weight or not.

BMI Categories

  • Under 18.5: Underweight
  • 18.5 - 24.9: Normal weight
  • 25 - 29.9: Overweight
  • 30 and above: Obesity
